Platform Features

Every feature built for your safety

From passive background protection to active emergency response — ShieldHer's smart features work in harmony to keep you safe.

📡
Live GPS Tracking
Real-time location sharing with trusted baateins. Auto-updates every 30 seconds. Works even in low-signal areas using satellite backup.
Core Safety
🚨
One-Touch SOS
Triple-press power button or use the in-app button. Alerts are dispatched to all emergency baateins and nearby volunteers within 3 seconds.
Emergency
🤖
AI Risk Assessment
Machine learning analyzes time, location, historical incident data, and crowd patterns to provide proactive safety scores for your route.

Silent Voice Activation
Activate SOS with a secret voice command. The app quietly records audio evidence and streams it to your baateins without alerting anyone nearby.

Crowdsourced incident reports create a live heat map of risk zones. Avoid danger before you encounter it with smart route suggestions.

Network

Emergency Services Link
Direct API integration with local police and hospitals. Automatically shares your medical profile and location with first responders.
